# App Store Listing Draft

**App Name:** MultiverseWiki: Rick & Morty DB

**Short Description:**
Explore the infinite multiverse! Your ultimate offline-ready guide to every character in the galaxy.

**Full Description:**
Dive into the world of Rick and Morty with MultiverseWiki. Whether you are a fan looking for hidden lore or just browsing, this app offers a complete database of characters.

**Key Features:**
* **Infinite Archive:** Access thousands of characters from the show.
* **Secret Dossiers:** Unlock AI-generated hidden stories for every character.
* **Offline Access:** Save your favorites and view them anywhere, even in Dimension C-137.
* **Instant Search:** Find any character in milliseconds.

**Keywords:**
rick and morty, wiki, database, characters, offline, cartoon, portal, multiverse.

**Privacy Policy Note:**
This app does not collect personal user data. All "Favorites" are stored locally on your device.
